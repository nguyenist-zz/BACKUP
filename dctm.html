ABOUT DC TECH MEETUP

The DC Tech Meetup convenes technologists, entrepreneurs, investors and the broader innovation community regularly to learn and share. Each month, 400+ innovators gather to see demos, launch products and meet their future co-founders, partners and funders. As one of the co-producers, I designed promotional posters for the event, usually themed by the month.
