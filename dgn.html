ABOUT DAILY GALAXY NOTES

I was inspired to sketch with the stylus once I got my Samsung Galaxy Note III. These daily illustrations are meant to visualize a morning news headline and provoke thought without an editorial slant. To view more illustrations visit my Tumblr.

One particular sketch is based off of the article, Why Google, Twitter and Tumblr are Backing the ‘Dancing Baby’. This Daily Galaxy Note was commissioned by the WordPress team to be illustrated on canvas and now has a home at WordPress HQ.
