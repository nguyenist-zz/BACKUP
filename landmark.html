Visual navigation without the maps.

ABOUT LANDMARK

Landmark is a mobile application for walking directions with step-by-step photos of geotagged landmarks. While most navigation applications are rooted in maps, a significant percentage of people prefer navigating visually, based on landmarks.

We're leveraging open source data (GPS, routing, points of interest) to create an app that allows you to get from point A to point B using photos & language specifically catered to pedestrian (4 blocks instead of 0.8 miles, Head toward Starbucks instead of Go NW). Our application and other platforms brings back the human element of intuitive directions.
