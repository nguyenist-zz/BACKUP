ABOUT STARTUP WEEKEND

Startup Weekend is very near and dear to me. I have attended 3 Startup Weekend events and have met both of my cofounders who were former teammates. I'm a fan and supporter and helped the organizers design the April 2014 event at WeWork Wonderbread Factory in downtown Washington D.C.

This weekend event garnered over 100 attendees. Local developers, designers, startup enthusiasts and more came together to pitch ideas for new startup companies and work to develop a working demo and presentation by Sunday. Startup Weekend shirts are unique to every city. I chose specific landmarks including the Washington Monument and the Lincoln Memorial and added a touch of the Cherry Blossom Festival since they were in full bloom.
