Wearably

Platform for media companies to distribute content through Google Glass.

ABOUT WEARABLY

We built a media platform, called Wearably, that allows publications to transform their RSS feeds into simple news-reading experiences for Glass. We worked on integrating news with partnerships from National Geographic, The Atlantic Media Company, Hacker News, and the New America Foundation.

How it works: We took the publisher's RSS feeds and automatically converted them into news "cards" that live on the platform. When a Glass user is flicking through the cards by swiping the frame above his right temple, he can stop on a particular story (usually displayed as a headline overlaid on a picture) and have it read out loud. Or, if it's just pictures, he can swipe through them at will.

Wearably was featured in publications such as Fast Company, Venture Beat and Pandodaily.
